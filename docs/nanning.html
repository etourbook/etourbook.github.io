Nanning

Nanning, the capital of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, is a vibrant city known for its lush greenery, rich ethnic culture, and strategic importance as a gateway to Southeast Asia. With a mix of modern development and traditional charm, Nanning offers visitors a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural experiences.
