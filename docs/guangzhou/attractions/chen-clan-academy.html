Chen Clan Ancestral Hall
Best Time to VisitDaytime
ActivitiesSightseeing
Crowd LevelCrowd

The Chen Clan Academy is one of the most representative buildings of the Lingnan region from the Qing Dynasty. More than just a clan ancestral hall, it is a microcosm of Lingnan culture. The exquisite carvings, paintings, and decorations reflect the pinnacle of craftsmanship at that time, with every brick and tile telling a story of history. Today, it serves as the Guangdong Folk Art Museum, showcasing the endless charm of traditional art to the world.
